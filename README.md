# NanyPet RestAPI

![alt text](https://i.ibb.co/yST3rtq/nanypet-logo-removebg-preview.png)

![alt text](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1tk8VA8wxVE0IxFXmurOuwEJwCBcEOkXX/view?usp=sharing)

This project is meant for educational purposes only.

RestAPI, manage basic CRUD operations for nanypet App, 3-tier Architecture, no controllers, DI(Dependency Injection) pattern, repository pattern, Ardalis Endpoints, caching, pagination.

There are many pet friendly places, however, some good places still do not allow pets. 
NanyPet brings you the opportunity to contact pet lovers who will gladly take care of your pet, while you enjoy a good restaurant with your family. movies, shopping or any other activity. 
NanyPet locates a sitter in your area, who for an agreed price will take care of your pet with responsibility and love. 
All with the security and identity validation of both owners and caretakers.
